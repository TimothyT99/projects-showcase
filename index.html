Live Crossing Status

Predict Level Crossing Closures & Plan Faster Journeys

Uses live train data from Realtime Trains to predict when the Lyminster level crossing barriers will drop, so drivers can time their journeys and avoid waiting. Calculates gate windows by offsetting train departure times from nearby stations. Integrates with Apple Shortcuts and Siri for hands-free status checks.
